Aibote Handmade Black Walnut Knife Sheath

Elevate your kitchen with this beautiful and functional Aibote black walnut knife sheath. Designed specifically for santoku knives, it offers a secure fit with a 5.0cm cutting edge entrance and supports blades up to 18cm in length. The elegant natural wood grain adds a touch of sophistication, while the durable construction ensures long-lasting use.

Inside, a high-quality magnet flannel lining protects your knife from scratches and ensures a smooth, damage-free storage experience. The convenient cork backing allows you to easily hang the sheath on the wall or tie it up, making it a practical and stylish addition to your kitchen decor.

With its moisture-resistant properties, this sheath is ideal for use with carbon steel knives, keeping them safe and secure for everyday cooking or professional use. Whether you're working in the kitchen or preparing for a meal, this knife sheath ensures your knife is always within reach and expertly protected.

A perfect blend of utility and design, this black walnut knife sheath is an essential tool for any chef who values both the performance and appearance of their kitchen tools.
