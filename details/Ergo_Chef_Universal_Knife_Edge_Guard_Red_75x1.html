Ergo Chef Universal Knife Edge Guard

Keep your knives safe and sharp with the Ergo Chef Universal Knife Edge Guard. This innovative solution is perfect for storing and transporting your cutlery, whether you're in the kitchen, traveling, or just keeping your tools organized. The guard's bright red color helps you quickly spot your knives, while its durable BPA-Free ABS plastic construction ensures long-lasting protection and strength.

The flared design allows for safe and secure insertion of your knife blade, preventing contact with other utensils and keeping sharp edges intact. With a size that fits blades up to 7.5 inches, it's ideal for a variety of knives, including chef's knives, paring knives, and more. This guard is not only a practical addition to your kitchen but also a smart way to extend the life of your knives and keep your workspace safer.

Designed with both functionality and convenience in mind, the Ergo Chef Knife Edge Guard is easy to clean and maintains hygiene after each use. Simply rinse with warm water and mild soap to keep it looking new and working effectively. A must-have for professionals, students, and home cooks who value quality and safety in their kitchen tools.
